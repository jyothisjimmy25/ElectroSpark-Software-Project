Full Wave Bridge Rectifier

Principle

The output of the rectifier contains d.c voltage and a small amount of d.c voltage. The ratio of the a.c component present across the load resistor to the value of d.c voltage gives the ripple factor.

                                                                                   Ripple factor = Va.c /Vd.c
